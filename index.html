Welcome to Cyber Safe Girl

Cyber Safe Girl is a unique program inspired by the Hon'ble Prime Minister Narendra Modi ji's "Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao" initiative. This project is "Beti Bachao Cyber Crime Se", designed to educate and empower about safe practices of responsible browsing and staying secure from emerging cyber threats, especially among the students, parents, and professionals.
